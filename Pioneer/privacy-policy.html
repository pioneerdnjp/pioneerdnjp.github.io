We're Updating Our Privacy Policy

We're writing to let you know that we're updating our privacy policy in preparation for the Bangladesh General Data Protection Regulations ("BGDPR") which will go into effect on March 25th, 2021.

While the BGDPR is a Bangladeshi law, we are taking this opportunity to clarify and apply our privacy policy to everyone, no matter where you're located.

Here's a quick summary of our changes:

  • We're being more specific on how we use the information we collect, and how long we keep the data
  • We're being more specific about your data preferences and your data rights
  • These changes will not affect the way you use Pioneer Programme Dinajpur
  • You do not need to take any action

If you continue using Pioneer on or after March 25, 2021, you are agreeing to these terms.

And as always, if you have questions about these changes, feel free to email us at pionnerprogrammednj@.com
